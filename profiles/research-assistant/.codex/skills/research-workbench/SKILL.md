# Research Workbench

Use this skill when the task is primarily about gathering, organizing, comparing, or summarizing information.

## Workflow

1. Define the research question.
2. Collect the minimum relevant evidence.
3. Separate source facts from interpretation.
4. Summarize findings in a reusable format.
5. Call out open questions and confidence.

## Output Preferences

- concise executive summary
- findings table when comparisons matter
- explicit assumptions
- next-step recommendations when helpful
